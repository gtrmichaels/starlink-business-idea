Executive Summary

This initiative establishes a high-speed internet network for 10 neighbors using a single Starlink kit, minimizing individual costs while ensuring reliable connectivity within our multi-story building.

Market Analysis

Target Market

Building residents requiring stable, high-speed internet.

Market Need

Neo-Kenya suffers from unreliable internet access. Starlink offers a solution bypassing traditional infrastructure limitations.

Business Model

Revenue

Monthly subscriptions from each user.

Costs

Starlink kit, network hardware, monthly Starlink service fee.
